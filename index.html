Projects

  • Legend of Zelda (Unity Rebuild)

Information: This is a remake of the legend of zelda (NES) which features the first dungeon and a custom level. Access the custom level by pressing the number 4 on your keyboard. Contribution: The enemies I worked on were: Stalfos, Keese, Blade Traps, Cannon Traps, Wallmasters, and Aquamentus. I designed the health and damage system, the knockback system and room transitions. I created the UI and pickup items for health, rupees, and bombs. Along with designing many of the rooms in the main dungeon, I also added a few of the custom level rooms. Lastly, I drew the cannon sprites and link's flying sprites. Technology: For this project, I used github and Unity with code written in C#. I also used generative AI for help with coding.

  • Roll A Ball (Unity Build)

Information: A short game where the player rolls a ball around to collect coins. The player can jump and fall off the edge. Collect all the coins to win the game. Contribution: I followed the Unity roll a ball tutorial. Technology: For this project, I used Unity with code written in C#. I also used generative AI for help with coding.
